Breaker list — clearer, off-aware rework

Five structurally different takes on the breaker list row. Every one (1) keeps the components visibly part of their breaker instead of a detached row below, (2) moves the actions to the upper-right corner, and (3) makes an off circuit read red at a glance. Each design is shown in both its ON and OFF state so you can compare the de-energized treatment directly. Tap "components" rows to expand.
